The Problem

Scam messages are becoming harder to identify.

Many scams now copy the tone of banks, delivery companies, and government services. This is especially stressful for elderly users aged 60 and above, but regular mobile users can also be caught when messages look urgent or official. A single wrong click can expose OTPs, passwords, NRIC details, or bank information.

Two Ways to Use KopiCheck

Choose the website chatbot or Telegram bot.

Paste a suspicious SMS, email, or link into the website chatbot, or send it to KopiCheck on Telegram. Both options give clear language to help users slow down before clicking or sharing personal details.

Simple Safety Steps

Stop. Check. Ask someone you trust.

  • Do not click links or reply when a message makes you feel rushed.
  • Never share OTPs, NRIC, Singpass passwords, or bank details.
  • Open official apps directly and ask a trusted person when you are unsure.
