The Problem

Scam messages are becoming harder to identify.

Many scams now copy the tone of banks, delivery companies, and government services. This is especially stressful for elderly users aged 60 and above, but regular mobile users can also be caught when messages look urgent or official. A single wrong click can expose OTPs, passwords, NRIC details, or bank information.

The Solution

KopiCheck gives quick, simple safety advice through the website chatbot and Telegram.

Users paste a suspicious SMS, email, or link into the website chatbot or Telegram bot. KopiCheck uses an AI-powered workflow to analyse the risk and reply in clear language, helping users slow down before clicking or sharing personal details.
