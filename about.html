About the project

About KopiCheck

KopiCheck is built to help users check suspicious messages before they click links, share sensitive information, or make unsafe decisions online.

Purpose

Helping users pause before risky online actions.

KopiCheck supports scam awareness by giving users a fast way to check suspicious messages. The project focuses on simple, calm guidance so users can pause, verify, and choose a safer next step.

Target Audience

Built for clarity and confidence

👵

Primary audience: elderly users aged 60 and above

Large text, simple steps, and practical advice help reduce confusion when suspicious messages appear.

📱

Secondary audience: adults aged 18 to 59

Regular mobile users can use KopiCheck as a quick second opinion when they encounter scams on SMS, email, or chat apps.

Project Disclaimer

Awareness support, not official advice

KopiCheck provides scam awareness support and does not replace official advice from banks, Singpass, government agencies, or law enforcement. For serious cases, users should contact official channels directly.
